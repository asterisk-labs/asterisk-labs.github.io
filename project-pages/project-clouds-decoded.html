Clouds Decoded, the first project undertaken by our new research laboratory, will revolutionise our understanding of turbulence, and phase heterogeneity in clouds in order to better understand the most pernicious and critical unknown in climate forecasts.

We see massive value in existing, yet neglected data sources; clouds are viewed by Earth observation scientists as an obstacle to be avoided or removed, with millions of cloudy images left to gather dust in the archive that are actually a goldmine of information
